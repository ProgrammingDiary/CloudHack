IDEA

To reduce the paper trail generated in the retail industry by creating a central bill storage location. This app creates a unique user dashboard which contains the bill details of all the purchases made by the user.

The app allows the user to get a list of all the service centres associated with that product via Google Maps and also a PDF format is created from the web form during the time of bill generation
